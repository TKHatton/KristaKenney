Hi, I'm Dr. Krista T. Kenney

I help women reignite their spark, reclaim their calm, and create a life where work and home don't have to compete. As a mom of three and a former high-level research executive, I know what burnout feels like—and what thriving looks like. Now, I use my training in neuroscience, public service, and spiritual leadership to guide others to a life marked by clarity, courage, and grace.
