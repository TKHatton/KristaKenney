From the Pages of Reignited

Chapter 1: The Burnout Epidemic

"Burnout isn't just feeling tired—it's a state of chronic stress that leads to emotional exhaustion, cynicism, and reduced effectiveness. And contrary to popular belief, it's not just about working too much. It's about working without boundaries, purpose, or the right support systems."

"In this chapter, we'll explore how to recognize the early warning signs of burnout before you reach crisis point, and why traditional advice to 'just take a vacation' rarely solves the underlying issues."

Chapter 4: Your Brain on Stress

"Understanding how your brain responds to chronic stress is the first step to reclaiming your calm. When your amygdala is constantly triggered, your prefrontal cortex—responsible for decision-making and emotional regulation—can't function optimally."

"This chapter provides practical neuroscience-based techniques to interrupt the stress cycle, regulate your nervous system, and restore cognitive function—even in the midst of demanding circumstances."

Chapter 7: Grace-Based Productivity

"The problem isn't that you're not doing enough—it's that you're trying to do everything. Grace-based productivity isn't about squeezing more into your day; it's about aligning your actions with your values and capacity."

"This chapter will help you create systems that honor your humanity, embrace imperfection, and still allow you to accomplish what matters most—without the crushing weight of hustle culture."
