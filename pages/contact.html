Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book you for speaking?

For major events (conferences, retreats, etc.), I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For smaller events or virtual presentations, 2-3 months notice is typically sufficient, though I occasionally have last-minute availability.

What are your coaching rates?

Coaching packages are customized based on your specific needs, goals, and preferred session frequency. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed proposal outlining investment options. All packages include session time, email support, and customized resources.

Do you offer virtual coaching?

Yes! All coaching services are available virtually via Zoom. This allows for flexibility in scheduling and eliminates geographic limitations. Virtual sessions are equally effective as in-person coaching and include the same level of personalized attention and resources.

Can I get a signed copy of your book?

Yes, signed copies of "Reignited" are available for purchase directly through this website. For bulk orders or special inscriptions, please mention this in your contact form submission, and we'll arrange the details.

Do you offer group discounts for coaching?

Yes, I offer special rates for organizations or groups interested in coaching for multiple team members. Group coaching provides a cost-effective way to bring evidence-based strategies to your entire team while building community and accountability.

What is your cancellation policy?

For coaching sessions, cancellations with less than 24 hours notice will be charged the full session fee. For speaking engagements, please refer to the terms outlined in your specific contract, as these vary based on event size and timing.
