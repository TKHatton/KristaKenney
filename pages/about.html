About Dr. Krista T. Kenney

Rooted in Grace. Driven by Excellence.

My Story

I've worn a lot of hats—leader, wife, mom, researcher, speaker—but the thread that ties them all together is my belief in showing up fully, even when it's hard. I'm an INFJ and a 4 on the Enneagram, which means I'm deeply empathetic, creative, and spiritually grounded—but I also see patterns, possibilities, and potential in others.

I believe in living a life that feels like you—not one you're performing in. Whether I'm coaching a working mom through burnout or speaking to a room full of professionals about emotional intelligence, I lead with compassion, clarity, and conviction.

My journey hasn't been a straight line. I've navigated high-pressure research environments, balanced family demands, and faced my own burnout. These experiences inform my approach to coaching and speaking—I don't just understand the theory, I've lived the reality.

Professional Background

With a Ph.D. in neuroscience and years of experience in both academic and corporate environments, I bring a unique perspective to my work. My research focused on how our brains respond to stress and how we can build resilience through evidence-based practices.

Before transitioning to coaching and speaking full-time, I served as a research executive, leading teams through complex projects while managing my own work-life integration. This hands-on leadership experience informs my practical approach to coaching.

I'm committed to ongoing education and hold certifications in:

  • Executive Coaching
  • Neuroscience-Based Leadership
  • Faith-Integrated Coaching
  • Trauma-Informed Practice

My Coaching Philosophy

Neuroscience-Informed

I integrate cutting-edge brain science into practical, accessible strategies. Understanding how your brain works is the first step to changing how you respond to stress and challenges.

Grace-Based

My approach is rooted in grace, not perfection. I believe in creating space for growth without shame or judgment, embracing both strengths and struggles as part of your journey.

Holistically Balanced

I don't separate work from home or mind from spirit. My coaching addresses the whole person, recognizing that true thriving happens when all aspects of life are in harmony.

Beyond the Bio

When I'm not speaking or coaching, you'll find me enjoying life with my family. I'm a devoted dog mom, an avid reader (ask me about my latest book obsession!), and I believe strongly in the restorative power of nature walks and good coffee.

My faith is central to who I am and how I approach my work. It informs my belief in the inherent value of each person and my commitment to serving others with integrity and compassion.

I'm also passionate about continuous learning and growth. I regularly attend conferences, participate in professional development, and engage with diverse perspectives to enhance my coaching and speaking.
