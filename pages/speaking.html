Popular Speaking Topics

Reignited: Overcoming Burnout Without Quitting Your Job

Based on her bestselling book, this talk provides a neuroscience-informed approach to recognizing, addressing, and preventing burnout. Audiences learn practical strategies for stress management, boundary-setting, and reclaiming joy in demanding seasons.

Perfect for: Corporate teams, healthcare professionals, educators, ministry leaders

The Integrated Life: Thriving at Work and Home

This talk challenges the myth of "work-life balance" and offers a more realistic framework for integration. Participants discover how to align priorities, manage energy (not just time), and create meaningful presence in all areas of life.

Perfect for: Working parents, professionals, women's conferences, leadership retreats

Brain-Based Leadership: Leading with Neuroscience and Grace

This presentation equips leaders with neuroscience-informed approaches to team management, communication, and organizational culture. Leaders learn how to foster psychological safety, facilitate growth, and lead with both effectiveness and compassion.

Perfect for: Executive teams, management training, leadership conferences

Finding Grace in the Gray: Navigating Life's Uncertainties

Based on her upcoming book, this talk addresses how to thrive during ambiguous seasons. Participants learn how uncertainty affects the brain and practical ways to cultivate resilience, make decisions, and find meaning in life's "gray areas."

Perfect for: Faith communities, personal development events, transition support groups

The Neuroscience of Connection: Building Meaningful Relationships

This presentation explores how our brains are wired for connection and the impact of relationships on our wellbeing. Audiences gain insights into communication patterns, conflict resolution, and creating deeper connections in a digital age.

Perfect for: Couples events, team building, community organizations

Custom Presentations

Krista develops customized talks based on your organization's specific needs and challenges. Each presentation is thoroughly researched and tailored to resonate with your unique audience while delivering practical, actionable content.
