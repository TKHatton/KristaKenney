Personalized guidance for thriving in every area of life

A Different Approach to Coaching

This coaching isn't about adding more to your plate—it's about helping you breathe again. I work with women, parents, and professionals who are doing 'all the things' but feel like they're losing themselves in the process.

You'll get a personalized plan that honors your reality—not your ideal calendar. We'll shift both your mindset and your systems using a unique blend of neuroscience and grace. This is coaching for people who want to thrive in their careers and their kitchens.

My approach is different because it's:

  • Evidence-based: Grounded in neuroscience research
  • Practical: Focused on real-world application
  • Holistic: Addressing work, home, and personal wellbeing
  • Grace-filled: Creating space for growth without perfectionism

Where Science Meets Spirit

My coaching blends brain-based tools with spiritually grounded practices—designed to create change that lasts without the hustle that drains you.

The Science

Understanding how your brain responds to stress, creates habits, and processes emotions is key to lasting change. I'll help you work with your brain's natural tendencies rather than fighting against them.

You'll learn practical neuroscience concepts like:

  • Stress response cycles and how to complete them
  • Neuroplasticity and habit formation
  • Emotional regulation techniques
  • Attention management in a distracted world

The Spirit

Technical knowledge alone isn't enough. True transformation happens when we align our actions with our deepest values and spiritual foundations.

We'll explore spiritual practices like:

  • Cultivating presence and mindfulness
  • Embracing grace over perfectionism
  • Finding purpose in daily work
  • Building meaningful connections

Frequently Asked Questions

How is coaching different from therapy?

While therapy often focuses on healing past wounds and addressing mental health concerns, coaching is forward-focused and action-oriented. We'll acknowledge your past experiences but primarily focus on creating strategies for future growth and success. I'm happy to work alongside your therapist if you're currently in therapy.

What can I expect in our first session?

Our initial session is all about understanding your current situation, challenges, and goals. I'll ask questions to get a comprehensive picture of your work, home life, and personal wellbeing. We'll identify key areas for growth and begin creating your personalized coaching plan. You'll leave with at least one practical strategy to implement right away.

How long does coaching typically last?

Most clients work with me for 3-6 months, though some continue for longer depending on their goals. We'll regularly assess progress and adjust our approach as needed. My goal is to equip you with the tools and mindset to thrive independently, not to create dependency.

Do you incorporate faith into your coaching?

My approach is informed by my faith, but I work with clients of all backgrounds and beliefs. I'll follow your lead regarding how much spiritual content you'd like incorporated into our sessions. My primary focus is creating a coaching experience that aligns with your values and supports your whole person.

Have more questions? I'd love to hear from you.
